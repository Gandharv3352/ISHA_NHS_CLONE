Our Journey

Where It All Began

Founded in 2020, ISHA set out to transform how healthcare appointments are managed. With a focus on A&E and general consultations, our system started as a university project and rapidly grew to support NHS facilities across the UK.

Milestones

  • 2020 – ISHA Founded by 4 university graduates
  • 2021 – First pilot run in a local NHS clinic
  • 2022 – Integrated SMS/email notifications
  • 2023 – Launched mobile-responsive portal
  • 2024 – Partnered with 50+ NHS facilities

Looking Ahead

Our goal is to leverage AI to predict patient needs, expand virtual care, and ensure every patient has instant access to timely appointments — no matter where they are.
