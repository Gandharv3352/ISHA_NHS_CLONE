How to Book an Appointment

Step 1: Visit the Booking Page

Login to the ISHA Health website and look for the "Book Appointment" button on your user dashboard. This will take you to the booking page where you can view all available doctors and time slots.

Step 2: Choose Your Doctor and Time

Browse the list of available doctors. Select the one that best suits your needs. After selecting your doctor, you will be prompted to choose an available date and time slot for your appointment.

Step 3: Enter Your Personal Details

Fill in your name, contact information, and any medical details requested. This information is required to complete your booking and ensure your appointment is scheduled correctly.

Step 4: Review and Confirm

After entering your details, review the booking summary. Make sure the doctor's name, time, and date are correct. If everything looks good, confirm your appointment.

Step 5: Receive Confirmation

Once your appointment is confirmed, you will receive a confirmation message on the website. Additionally, a confirmation email will be sent to you with the appointment details.
