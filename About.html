Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register with a GP (General Practitioner)?
You can register by contacting your local GP surgery directly or by using the NHS online registration service. You'll usually need proof of address and identification.
What should I do in a medical emergency?
In an emergency, call 999 immediately or visit your nearest A&E (Accident & Emergency) department. For urgent but non-life-threatening issues, call 111.
Are NHS services free for everyone?
Most NHS services are free for UK residents. However, charges may apply for prescriptions, dental care, or if you're not ordinarily resident in the UK.
How can I get a repeat prescription?
You can request a repeat prescription through your GP surgery's online system, the NHS App, or by contacting the surgery directly.
