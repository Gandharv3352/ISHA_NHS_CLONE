Privacy Policies

1. Introduction

At ISHA Healthcare, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in accordance with the UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and relevant healthcare privacy frameworks.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name, contact details, and demographic information
  • Medical history and appointment records
  • Website usage data (via cookies and analytics)
  • Form submissions and feedback

3. Purpose of Data Collection

Your data is used to manage appointments, respond to queries, improve services, and meet legal healthcare requirements. We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

  • Consent
  • Contractual necessity
  • Legal obligations
  • Vital interest in emergencies

5. Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored securely within UK servers using encrypted technologies. NHS and GDPR retention policies are followed: patient data for 8+ years, children's records until age 25, and web queries for 12 months.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

  • Access, correct, or erase your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent anytime

Email us at: privacy@ishaclinic.uk

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential and analytical cookies. You can disable them through your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for more info.

8. Data Sharing and Security

Data is only shared with authorized medical or technical staff. We use SSL, access control, and audit logs. Our future plan includes edge-AI and federated learning-based security.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16 without parental consent.

10. Contact Us

Email: privacy@ishaclinic.uk
Address: ISHA Clinic, 123 Healing Street, London, UK
